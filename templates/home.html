Weather Data API

Endpoints:

  • Get temperature from a station on a date: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/station/date
  • Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/10/1988-10-25
  • Get all data from a station: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/station
  • Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/10
  • Get all data from a station in a specific year: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/yearly/station/year
  • Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/yearly/10/1988
